We investigate the complex landscape of skin aging by integrating multi-omics data from our unique **longevity population cohorts**.

**Key Areas:**

- Identifying novel biomarkers of aging
- Exploring the role of cellular senescence in the skin microenvironment
- Validating potent anti-aging compounds using advanced _in vivo_ and _in vitro_ models
