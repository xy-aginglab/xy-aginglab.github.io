Meet the Team

Members of the Li Lab

Principal Investigator

Ji Li 李吉

Vice President of Xiangya Hospital, Professor, Doctoral Supervisor

Vice President of Xiangya Hospital, Central South University. Director of Hunan Key Laboratory of Aging Biology. Postdoctoral fellow at University of Southern California. Recipient of the National Science Fund for Distinguished Young Scholars, MOE Young Changjiang Scholar.

Currently serves as Chair of the Rosacea Group, Chinese Medical Doctor Association Dermatology; Deputy Chair of the Trichology Group, CMA Dermatology; Vice Chair of the CMDA Postgraduate Medical Education Standards Committee; and Chair-Elect of the Hunan Medical Association Committee on Medicine & Aesthetics.

Research focus on pilosebaceous-related diseases (rosacea, alopecia) and skin aging — basic, clinical, and translational research. Led 1 National Key R&D Program and 6 NSFC projects. Published 120+ SCI papers as first/corresponding author in Cell, BMJ, Sci Adv, Dev Cell, EMBO J, JAAD, JAMA Dermatol, JID, etc. Led the first Chinese Expert Consensus and Guidelines on Rosacea. Holder of 5 national invention patents.

Deputy Editor-in-Chief of the National Standardized Residency Training Textbook "Dermatology and Venereology" (3rd ed.), Associate Editor of Sensory Neuroscience.

Recipient of the Hunan Science & Technology Progress Award (1st Prize, 2021), Huaxia Medical Science & Technology Award (1st Prize, 2021), CMA Medical Science & Technology Award (1st Prize, 2023), and the Wuzhou Women's Science & Technology Award.
