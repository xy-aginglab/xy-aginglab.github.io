Hunan Key Laboratory of Aging Biology

Xiangya Hospital, Central South University · Department of Dermatology

Our laboratory operates at the intersection of clinical dermatology and molecular biology, leveraging the vast clinical resources of Xiangya Hospital to drive translational breakthroughs. Under the direction of Prof. Ji Li, we are dedicated to uncovering the biological hallmarks of skin health and developing next-generation interventions.

Research Directions

Skin Aging

Investigating the mechanisms of skin aging using longevity cohorts, animal models, and cell models, with targeted anti-aging drug design and development for key molecular targets.

Learn more

Hair Diseases & Hair Regeneration

Integrating clinical resources with human hair follicle organ culture models to study the pathogenesis of alopecia and hair graying, develop targeted therapeutics, and establish stem cell-based hair follicle reconstruction technologies.

Learn more

Inflammatory Skin Diseases

Integrating clinical resources with animal and cell models to investigate the pathogenesis of inflammatory skin diseases — particularly disfiguring conditions such as rosacea and acne — and developing targeted therapeutic drugs for key molecular pathways.

Learn more
