Li Lab

Xiangya Hospital, Central South University · Department of Dermatology

Our laboratory operates at the intersection of clinical dermatology and molecular biology, leveraging the vast clinical resources of Xiangya Hospital to drive translational breakthroughs. Under the direction of Prof. Ji Li, we are dedicated to uncovering the biological hallmarks of skin health and developing next-generation interventions.

Research Directions

Pilosebaceous-Related Diseases

Studying the pathogenesis of alopecia, hair graying, rosacea, and acne, and developing targeted therapeutics and hair follicle reconstruction technologies.

Learn more

Skin Aging

Investigating the mechanisms of skin aging using longevity cohorts, animal models, and cell models, with targeted anti-aging drug design and development for key molecular targets.
