Dan Jian

简丹

Professor, Doctoral Supervisor

Department of Dermatology

Visiting Scholar at University of Southern California (2013).

Serves on the 8th Editorial Board of Chinese Journal of Aesthetic Medicine, and as Standing Committee Member of the Anti-Aging Medicine Committee, the Precision & Digital Medicine Branch of the Chinese Association of Plastics and Aesthetics, and the Women's Dermatology Committee of the Hunan Women Physicians Association.

Research focuses on the clinical and basic research of rosacea, as well as skin aging mechanisms. Led 1 NSFC Young Scientists Fund, 4 NSFC General Programs, and 2 Hunan Natural Science Foundation projects. Made significant contributions to rosacea clinical management and molecular mechanism research.

Contributed to the Chinese Rosacea Diagnostic Criteria, co-authored the Chinese Rosacea Diagnosis and Treatment Guidelines, and participated in the preparation of Establishment and Application of the Chinese Rosacea Diagnosis and Treatment System and Expert Consensus on Intradermal Botulinum Toxin Type A for Persistent Erythema and Flushing in Rosacea. Contributing author of the monograph Rosacea. Published over 40 peer-reviewed papers domestically and internationally.

Recipient of the "Outstanding Physician Award" from the Hunan Medical Plastic and Aesthetics Association and the 2017 CDA "Outstanding Young and Middle-aged Dermatologist of China" Award. Principal translator of 3 monographs including Intense Pulsed Light in Medical Aesthetics.
