<div class="bloom-hero">
  <h1>Welcome to the Aging Biology Lab</h1>
  <p class="bloom-hero__tagline">where we study the mechanisms of skin aging, hair follicle regeneration, and inflammatory skin diseases.</p>

  <div class="bloom-hero__body">
    Our laboratory operates at the intersection of <strong>clinical dermatology</strong> and
    <strong>molecular biology</strong>, leveraging the vast clinical resources of
    <strong>Xiangya Hospital</strong> to drive translational breakthroughs.
    Under the direction of <strong>Prof. Ji Li</strong>, we employ experimental and
    computational techniques to uncover the biological hallmarks of skin health
    and develop next-generation interventions.
  </div>

<div class="bloom-section">
  <h2>Research Directions</h2>
  <ul class="bloom-research-list">
    <li>
      <h3>Skin Aging & Longevity</h3>
      <p>Integrating multi-omics data from longevity cohorts to identify novel biomarkers, explore cellular senescence, and validate anti-aging compounds.</p>
    </li>
    <li>
      <h3>Hair Follicle Biology</h3>
      <p>Deciphering hair growth and regeneration mechanisms, pioneering stem cell-based reconstruction and small-molecule therapies for alopecia.</p>
    </li>
    <li>
      <h3>Inflammatory Skin Diseases</h3>
      <p>Characterizing immune-microbial interactions to develop precise, targeted therapeutic strategies for rosacea, acne, and related conditions.</p>
    </li>
  </ul>
</div>
