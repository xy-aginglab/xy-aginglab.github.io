  <div class="bloom-home__body">
    <p>
      In the <strong>Aging Biology Lab</strong>, we study the mechanisms of skin aging and
      develop next-generation interventions. We use experimental and computational techniques
      to understand the molecular hallmarks of skin health, and how these insights can be
      translated into clinical applications for dermatological diseases.
    </p>
    <p>
      Our lab is part of the
      <a href="https://www.xiangya.com.cn/">Department of Dermatology</a>
      at <a href="https://www.csu.edu.cn/">Xiangya Hospital, Central South University</a>.
      We are also affiliated with the
      <a href="https://www.csu.edu.cn/">Hunan Key Laboratory of Aging Biology</a>
      and the National Clinical Research Center for Geriatric Disorders.
      Prof. Ji Li is the director of the laboratory.
    </p>
