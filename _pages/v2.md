      <p>Our laboratory sits at the intersection of clinical dermatology and molecular biology. We decode the pathogenic mechanisms of <i>pilosebaceous-related diseases</i> — including <strong>rosacea</strong>, <strong>hair loss</strong>, and <strong>hair graying</strong> — and investigate the fundamental processes of <i>skin aging</i>. By integrating patient cohorts, multi-omics profiling, disease models, and translational experiments, we develop targeted therapies that bridge bench discovery with bedside application.</p>
      <p>High-impact discoveries published in <em>Cell</em>, <em>Nature Medicine</em>, <em>Nature Communications</em>, <em>Science Advances</em>, and <em>JAAD</em> have advanced our understanding from molecular mechanisms to clinical interventions across both research directions.</p>
