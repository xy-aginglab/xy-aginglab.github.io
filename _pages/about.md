Our laboratory operates at the intersection of **clinical dermatology** and **molecular biology**, leveraging the vast clinical resources of **Xiangya Hospital** to drive translational breakthroughs. We are dedicated to uncovering the biological hallmarks of skin health and developing next-generation interventions.

### Research Interests

#### 1. Mechanisms of Skin Aging & Longevity

We investigate the complex landscape of skin aging by integrating multi-omics data from our unique **longevity population cohorts**.

- **Focus:** Identifying novel biomarkers of aging, exploring the role of cellular senescence in the skin microenvironment, and validating potent anti-aging compounds using advanced _in vivo_ and _in vitro_ models.

#### 2. Hair Follicle Biology & Regenerative Medicine

Our team deciphers the molecular logic governing hair growth and regeneration to combat diverse alopecia and pigmentary disorders.

- **Focus:** Utilizing high-fidelity clinical data and hair follicle organ culture systems to study pathogenesis. We are pioneering stem cell-based hair follicle reconstruction and the development of targeted small-molecule drugs for hair regrowth and graying reversal.

#### 3. Pathogenesis of Inflammatory & Disfiguring Skin Diseases

We aim to bridge the gap between basic immunology and clinical dermatology for prevalent conditions such as **rosacea and acne**.

- **Focus:** Characterizing the immune-microbial interactions on the skin surface and identifying key inflammatory pathways. Our goal is to translate these mechanistic insights into precise, targeted therapeutic strategies for patients.
