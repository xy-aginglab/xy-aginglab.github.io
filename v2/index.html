Welcome to the Aging Biology Lab

where we study the mechanisms of skin aging, hair follicle regeneration, and inflammatory skin diseases.

In the Aging Biology Lab, we study the mechanisms of skin aging and develop next-generation interventions. We use experimental and computational techniques to understand the molecular hallmarks of skin health, and how these insights can be translated into clinical applications for dermatological diseases.

Our lab is part of the Department of Dermatology at Xiangya Hospital, Central South University. We are also affiliated with the Hunan Key Laboratory of Aging Biology and the National Clinical Research Center for Geriatric Disorders. Prof. Ji Li is the director of the laboratory.
