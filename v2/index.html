Welcome to the Aging Biology Lab

where we study the mechanisms of skin aging, hair follicle regeneration, and inflammatory skin diseases.

Our laboratory operates at the intersection of clinical dermatology and molecular biology, leveraging the vast clinical resources of Xiangya Hospital to drive translational breakthroughs. Under the direction of Prof. Ji Li, we employ experimental and computational techniques to uncover the biological hallmarks of skin health and develop next-generation interventions.
Learn more about our research

Research Directions

  • Skin Aging & Longevity

    Integrating multi-omics data from longevity cohorts to identify novel biomarkers, explore cellular senescence, and validate anti-aging compounds.

  • Hair Follicle Biology

    Deciphering hair growth and regeneration mechanisms, pioneering stem cell-based reconstruction and small-molecule therapies for alopecia.

  • Inflammatory Skin Diseases

    Characterizing immune-microbial interactions to develop precise, targeted therapeutic strategies for rosacea, acne, and related conditions.
