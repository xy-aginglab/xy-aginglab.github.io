Welcome to the Li Lab

where we study the mechanisms of pilosebaceous-related diseases

In the Li Lab, we study pilosebaceous-related diseases and skin aging. We use experimental and computational techniques to understand the molecular mechanisms of hair disorders, inflammatory skin conditions, and aging, and translate these insights into clinical applications.

Our lab is part of the Department of Dermatology at Xiangya Hospital, Central South University. We are also affiliated with the Li Lab and the National Clinical Research Center for Geriatric Disorders. Prof. Ji Li is the director of the laboratory.
